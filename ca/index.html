Meet Chef Chen

Born in Wuhu, China and raised in Nashville, Tennessee, Chef Chen Chen grew up immersed in two culinary worlds — his family's Chinese restaurant and the bold, warm hospitality of Southern comfort food.

After cooking for thousands of festival-goers at Bonnaroo and studying culinary arts at George Brown College in Toronto, Chen fused his roots into something new: Nashville hot chicken elevated with Szechuan heat, hand-breaded and fried to precise temperatures, finished with a custom chili oil infusion that balances heat, sweet, and spice.

His philosophy is simple: do simple things exceptionally well.

Our Chef

Born in China and raised in Nashville, Chen Chen grew up surrounded by the bold flavors and warm hospitality that define Southern comfort food. After years in Nashville's vibrant food scene and studying culinary arts and entrepreneurship, Chen built his vision around one idea — authentic Nashville hot chicken with a chef-inspired Asian twist.

His journey wasn't a straight line. Before opening his own kitchen, Chen cooked at the Bonnaroo Music & Arts Festival, where his team served food around the clock to thousands — an experience that taught him discipline, consistency, and the power of great food to bring people together.

Now Chen Chen's is bringing it back to where it all started — the United States. Each dish reflects Chen's belief in doing simple things exceptionally well: fried chicken that's crisp, juicy, and full of flavor, served with the warmth and hospitality that inspired it all.
